Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information you provide to us when using Spendly:

  • Account information (email, password)
  • Expense data you enter (amount, category, date, description)
  • Usage data to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To store and display your expense records
  • To generate spending insights and summaries
  • To authenticate your account
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. You retain full control over your data and can request deletion at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • With service providers who host our servers (bound by confidentiality agreements)

We do not sell or rent your data to any third party.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session and preferences. These cookies are necessary for the app to function properly.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app.
